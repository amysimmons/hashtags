If you try searching for #lesbians, #bi or #gays on Instagram, you won’t get much luck.

The Instagram hashtag, which groups together popular things and trends, brings up the message ‘No Posts Yet’ every time you try searching those terms.

You’d think they were being homophobic, but it’s the opposite actually.

It’s part of Instagram’s attempt to fight porn on the site by banning certain hashtags associated with that.

Part of the terms banned are the obvious ones too, like #anal, #breast and #penis.

But the far less obvious banned ones are #lesbiansofinstagram, #snapchatgay and #iamgay.

This is surprising because, according to a new study, LGB people are more than twice as likely as straight people to use Instagram or Twitter, so #iamgay would be a popular hashtag being searched.

Fortunately, there’s a few that are still safe (phew), such as #queer#bisexual#gaylove and #transgender.

So don’t forget to use those next time you want to share pictures with you and your bae.
